About us



SSF India is a dynamic and proactive organization committed to nurturing the educational and holistic growth of students across the country. Through a range of initiatives, including academic support, skill development, leadership opportunities, and community engagement, SSF India aims to empower students to excel in their academic pursuits while also fostering a sense of social responsibility and ethical values. As the Sunni Student Federation, we are dedicated to promoting a balanced and comprehensive approach to education, instilling both academic excellence and moral integrity in the next generation of leaders and thinkers..


SSF JMI is a dedicated student-led initiative that strives to provide comprehensive support to aspiring individuals seeking admission to Jamia Millia Islamia University. Our services encompass an Admission Help Desk, offering personalized guidance and assistance, alongside the provision of free study materials and past year question papers to aid in exam preparation. We further enhance the academic journey through informative talks and discussions, fostering a dynamic learning environment. Additionally, SSF JMI facilitates spiritual gatherings that offer a space for introspection and communal unity. As the Sunni Student Federation, we are committed to the holistic development of students, combining academic excellence with spiritual growth to empower individuals on their educational path.
