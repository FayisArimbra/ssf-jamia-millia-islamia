  • Establishment: Jamia Millia Islamia was founded in 1920 during the Indian freedom movement by prominent Muslim leaders, including Maulana Mohammad Ali Jauhar, Maulana Shaukat Ali, and Hakim Ajmal Khan.
  • Central University: is a Central University, which means it is funded and administered by the Indian government through the University Grants Commission (UGC).
  • Location The university is located in New Delhi, the capital city of India. Its campus is situated in the Jamia Nagar area of South Delhi.
  • Academic Programs: JMI offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines, including arts, science, commerce, engineering, law, education, social sciences, and more.
  • Environment: JMI is known for its diverse and multicultural campus, attracting students from different parts of India and various countries, making it a vibrant and enriching academic environment.
  • Research and Innovation: university is actively engaged in research and innovation across different disciplines. It encourages its faculty and students to participate in research activities and contribute to academic advancements.
